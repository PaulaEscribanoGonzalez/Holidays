What is Boston Cream Pie Day?

Every year on October 23rd, dessert lovers unite to celebrate the creamy, chocolatey, and custard-filled wonder known as the Boston Cream Pie. The History

The Boston Cream Pie was first created in 1856 by Armenian-French chef M. Sanzian at the Parker House Hotel in Boston, Massachusetts. At that time, chocolate frosting was a rare treat, and this dessert quickly became a sensation.

How to Celebrate

Enjoy a slice of Boston Cream Pie at your favorite bakery, try baking your own, or visit the original Parker House Hotel to taste the authentic version. Share your creations on social media using #BostonCreamPieDay!
